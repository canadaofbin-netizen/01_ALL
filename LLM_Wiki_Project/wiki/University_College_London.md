# University College London (UCL)

University College London (UCL) is an academic institution in London, UK. 

In the context of the [[2027_Resume_Summary]], it is the institution where the subject is studying for a BSc in Psychology and Language Sciences (September 2025 – June 2028). The coursework includes Cognitive Psychology, Neuroscience, Language Acquisition, and Statistical Methods.

## BCI & Neurotechnology Research
UCL's Department of Speech, Hearing & Phonetic Sciences houses the [[Iverson_Lab]], led by Professor Paul Iverson, which is developing an open-source dry-electrode wearable [[Electroencephalography]] system with an integrated microphone-array for ecological speech perception research. This project is funded by the UK Economic and Social Research Council (ESRC). The lab's research combines hardware innovation with LLM-based neural tracking analysis, bridging [[Brain_Computer_Interface]] technology with real-world language acquisition studies.

UCL is also connected to the wider UK neurotech ecosystem via [[Cambridge_NeuroWorks]], where clinical specialists from UCLH (e.g., neurosurgeon Harith Akram) participate in the innovation community.
