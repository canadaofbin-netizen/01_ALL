# Neural Digital Twin (NDT)

A Neural Digital Twin (NDT) is a dynamic, highly personalized computational model of a user's brain-BCI interaction, hosted in the cloud.

## Solving Neuroplastic Drift
By continuously updating a sophisticated neural profile via real-time telemetry from wearables, NDTs solve the issue of neuroplastic drift. This enables [[Brain_Computer_Interface]] systems to adapt seamlessly to changing baseline cognitive states over time, overcoming the historical requirement for daily recalibration and moving the industry toward true "plug-and-play" functionality.
