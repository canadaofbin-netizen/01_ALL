# Apple

Apple maintains significant R&D centers in London and Cambridge, focusing heavily on Human Factors, AIML, and wearable health technologies (e.g., AirPods with EEG sensors).

## Application Strategy
- **Value Proposition**: Pitch the lateral-channel EEG decoding project, which aligns perfectly with Apple's philosophy of 'seamless' design (avoiding bulky headbands). Highlight experience in cleaning noisy raw data for on-device neural engines.
- **Networking**: Apple's strict secrecy means direct lab contact is difficult. Target UK University Recruiters on LinkedIn for AIML/HMI internship timelines.
