# Job Insecurity

Job insecurity is the subjective appraisal that one's current employment is threatened. It can lead to severe downstream consequences such as burnout and [[Counterproductive_Work_Behavior]].

## Technological Threats
Applying the cognitive appraisal theory of stress, Yam et al. (2023) found that the mere presence or thought of robots in the workplace acts as a threatening environmental stimulus, leading to goal-incongruent appraisals and heightened job insecurity, independent of actual job loss rates. Notably, simple psychological interventions, such as self-affirmation exercises, can build a "flexible self-system" that buffers these negative appraisals.
