# Kim Lab Journal Club Summer 2026

This document summarizes the reading list and presentation guide for the [[Kim_Lab]] Journal Club scheduled for Summer 2026.

## Structure and Themes
The club requires two empirical article summaries per week (5-10 minutes each). Presenters must follow a strict 10-minute structure covering: Title/Motivation, Implications, Research Design, Key Results, and Takeaways.

The curriculum is divided into two primary themes:
1. **Weeks 1-2**: Traditional and Nontraditional Studies of [[Situational_Strength]]. It covers how situation strength and [[Trait_Activation_Theory]] predict job performance and strain.
2. **Weeks 3-6**: [[AI_and_Work]]. This covers foundations, interactions with personality, trust, [[Algorithm_Aversion]], and [[Algorithmic_Management]].
