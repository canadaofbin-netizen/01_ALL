# Summer 2027 Internship Networking

This summary aggregates strategies and timelines for securing a Research or Engineering internship in the neurotechnology and spatial computing sectors for Summer 2027.

## Key Strategies
1. **Highlighting Hybrid Competencies**: The core strategy is to emphasize the rare combination of cognitive science (PALS) and data engineering. Specifically, demonstrating the ability to build automated data pipelines (e.g., bypassing bot detection for 600+ papers) and translating complex EEG signals (e.g., motor imagery decoding using lateral channels T3, T4).
2. **Targeting Form-Factor Realism**: Presenting research that aligns with the physical constraints of smart glasses and VR headsets, specifically focusing on lateral and frontal EEG channels rather than the primary motor cortex (C3, C4) which is difficult to access with consumer wearables.
