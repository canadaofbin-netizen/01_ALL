# Neurotech Hackathons

Hackathons provide critical opportunities to build practical portfolios, demonstrate collaborative coding skills, and network with industry professionals.

## Key Events Timeline

### Summer 2026
- **MLH Global Hack Week (July)**: A 100% online festival focused on learning and portfolio building. Offers excellent tutorials on data cleaning, API integration, and basic machine learning, making it an ideal starting point for collaborative coding.

### Fall 2026
- **BigBrain Workshop (October)**: A major academic seminar focusing on high-resolution 3D brain mapping. Demonstrates the integration of anatomical brain structures with AI.
- **Brainhack Global (Nov-Dec)**: A collaborative event focused on open-source neuroscience data. Features a 'TrainTrack' for learning Python/R and AI basics for neuro-data, and a 'HackTrack' for executing mini-projects with fMRI/EEG data.

### Spring/Summer 2027
- **The London Neurotech Hackathon (Jan-Feb)**: A major UK event led by students from UCL and Imperial, focusing heavily on BCI and EEG data. Highly regarded by neurotech companies.
- **Imperial College Closed-Loop Neurotechnology Hackathon**: Focuses on real-time EEG analysis and closed-loop stimulation systems.
- **BR41N.IO BCI Hackathon & Spring School (April)**: Combines intensive neurotechnology seminars with team projects.
- **VibeHack London (June)**: A UCL-based hackathon exploring AI, mental wellness, and cognitive assistive tools.
