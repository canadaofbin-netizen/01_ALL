# Meta-Analysis

Meta-analysis is a quantitative, formal, epidemiological study design used to systematically assess previous research studies to derive conclusions about that body of research. It involves extracting statistical metrics (like correlation coefficients and Cronbach's alpha) from many papers to synthesize findings.
