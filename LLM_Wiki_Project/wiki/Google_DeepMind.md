# Google DeepMind

DeepMind is a top-tier target for Research Engineering (RE) roles. They highly value engineers who possess deep domain knowledge in psychology and neuroscience.

## Application Strategy
- **Value Proposition**: Emphasize 'practical data engineering' (e.g., automated data collection pipelines) combined with cognitive science expertise.
- **Networking**: Leverage the extensive UCL alumni network (especially from PALS and the Gatsby Unit) working at DeepMind. Reach out via LinkedIn for coffee chats.
