# Reproducibility

Reproducibility refers to the ability to duplicate research findings. Psychology and neuroscience have faced a "reproducibility crisis" where many seminal effects failed to replicate.

## Key Issues
Questionable Research Practices (QRPs) such as p-hacking (massaging data to get p<.05), HARKing (Hypothesizing After Results are Known), publication bias, and low statistical power severely degrade the Positive Predictive Value of published research.

## Open Science Reforms
Promoting [[Research_Process]] transparency through:
- **Preregistration**: Locking in hypotheses and methods prior to data collection.
- **Open Data & Code**: Facilitating external audits and exact re-analysis.
