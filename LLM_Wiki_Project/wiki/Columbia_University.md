# Columbia University

## Background
Columbia University is a private Ivy League research university in New York City, USA. It houses the Zuckerman Mind Brain Behavior Institute and the Department of Electrical Engineering.

## Key Attributes
Home to the [[NAPLab]] (Neural Acoustic Processing Lab), led by Professor Nima Mesgarani, which is pioneering [[Auditory_Attention_Decoding]] (AAD) and brain-controlled hearing technology. The lab's groundbreaking research on real-time brain-controlled selective hearing was published in Nature Neuroscience in 2026, achieving up to 90% accuracy in attention decoding using iEEG and a Mamba-TasNet architecture. NAPLab also conducts convergence research between LLMs and human auditory cortex information encoding, published in Nature Machine Intelligence (2024, 2026).
