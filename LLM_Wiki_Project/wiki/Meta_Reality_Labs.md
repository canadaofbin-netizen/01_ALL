# Meta Reality Labs

Meta Reality Labs (specifically the London division) is a primary target for Research Scientist and HCI internships. They focus heavily on neuromotor interfaces (e.g., wrist-worn EMG) and AR/VR integration.

## Application Strategy
- **Timeline**: Extremely early (August to October for the following Summer).
- **Networking**: Direct emails are often rejected by ATS. Apply via the portal first, then send paper-focused outreach to Lab Leads (e.g., Prof. Alexandre Gramfort) via LinkedIn or email.
