# Sony Interactive Entertainment

The SIE Future Technology Group and London Studio focus on immersive AR/VR experiences (PSVR2) and next-generation HCI (eye-tracking, biosignals).

## Application Strategy
- **Value Proposition**: Frame BCI/EEG research not just as academic inquiry, but as a next-generation controller input for gaming. The VR headset form-factor is perfectly suited for lateral/frontal EEG channels.
- **Networking**: Target HCI Researchers and R&D Engineers in London. Look for 'Student Placement' or summer intern roles.
