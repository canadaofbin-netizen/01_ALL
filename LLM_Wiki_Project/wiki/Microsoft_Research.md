# Microsoft Research

Microsoft Research (MSR) Cambridge is a premier destination for 12-week academic research internships focusing on Mixed Reality (HoloLens), HCI, and inclusive design/accessibility.

## Application Strategy
- **Value Proposition**: Emphasize automated data pipelines (highly valued for accelerating research) and frame the lateral-channel EEG project as an accessibility and cognitive load solution for MR headsets.
- **Networking**: Email the official MSR Cambridge academic inbox (`msrcam@microsoft.com`). Connect with HCI Researchers or Cognitive Scientists on LinkedIn.
