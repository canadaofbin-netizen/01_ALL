# Ultraleap

Ultraleap (based in Bristol and London) is a leader in optical hand tracking and mid-air haptic feedback for XR interfaces.

## Application Strategy
- **Value Proposition**: Propose a multi-modal HCI approach: combining Ultraleap's hand tracking for spatial pointing with BCI for cognitive intent (the "click"), thereby eliminating the "Midas Touch" false-positive problem.
- **Networking**: Since it is a mid-sized company, direct cold emails to Machine Learning Leads or HCI Researchers are highly effective.
